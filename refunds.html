Refunds & Returns Policy

_Last updated: September 12, 2025_

This page explains how refunds work for Elizabeth N Exclusives Market Hub (“EN Exclusives”). It summarizes key points from our Terms of Service and is tailored to digital products.

Plain-English summary: Digital downloads are delivered instantly and generally non‑refundable once accessed. If something is wrong (e.g., corrupted file, not as described), contact us quickly—we’ll help fix it or consider a good‑faith refund if eligible.

1) Scope

This policy applies to digital products purchased on our website (e.g., PDFs, templates, spreadsheets, guides). For mobile apps purchased via third‑party app stores, please see the Apps & Third‑Party Stores section below.

2) Digital products — general rule

  • Because digital items are available immediately, all sales are final once a file or link has been accessed or downloaded, to the extent permitted by law.
  • If you have not accessed or downloaded the item and want to cancel, contact us within 7 days of purchase.

3) If there’s a problem with your files

We’ll fix access issues wherever possible. You may be eligible for a refund or exchange if:

  • Files are corrupted or unopenable, and we cannot provide a working replacement promptly; or
  • The product is materially not as described on its page; and
  • You contact us within 7 days of purchase and have not used or publicly shared the files.
We may request reasonable proof (e.g., screenshots, error messages) to resolve the issue.

4) How to request a refund or exchange

Email lizukandu@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: Refund/Exchange Request — Order # and Product Name
  • *Your name and email used for purchase
  • *Order number and purchase date
  • Description of the issue (and screenshots if available)

We aim to reply within 2–4 business days.

5) Exchanges

When appropriate, we may:

  • Replace the file with an updated/corrected version; or
  • Credit you toward a different product of equal/lower value.

6) Apps & third‑party stores

If you obtained an app through a third‑party app store (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store), the store’s refund rules apply. We can assist with troubleshooting but cannot process refunds for third‑party store purchases.

7) Exceptions (examples)

Refunds are generally not available for:

  • Change of mind after access/download
  • Incompatibility due to unsupported or outdated software/hardware when the product page lists requirements
  • Purchases made using promotional codes after those promotions expire
  • Items obtained via unauthorized resellers or shared links

Your statutory consumer rights (where applicable) remain unaffected.

8) Chargebacks

If you have an issue, please contact us first—we’re happy to help. Unauthorized or inappropriate chargebacks may result in account limitations while we investigate.

9) Processing time

Approved refunds are typically processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

10) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always appear on this page.
