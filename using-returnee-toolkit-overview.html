How do I use the Returnee Toolkit?

The Returnee Toolkit includes a full checklist and phase pages to guide your move back home.

Where to start

  • **Splash/index page:** links to all phases.
  • **Full Checklist:** `/returnee-checklist.html` (complete, searchable).
  • **Phases:** `/before-you-fly.html`, `/first-week.html`, `/first-month.html`.

Features

  • Checkboxes that save in your browser
  • Notes per section
  • Progress bar + Export/Import your progress
  • Print‑friendly version

Tip

Bookmark the pages on your phone for offline access.
