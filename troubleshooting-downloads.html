My download failed or won’t open

Quick checks

  • Try a stable Wi‑Fi connection (hotspot if needed).
  • Save the file to your device first, then open from Files/Downloads.
  • Use **Chrome** or **Safari** for HTML tools.

If it still fails

  1. Clear your browser cache.
  2. Try another browser or device.
  3. Sign in and re‑download from **My Account → Orders**.
  4. If you purchased as a guest, search your inbox for our receipt email and use the links again.

File‑type notes

  • **.html** tools (e.g., Meal Planner) run offline in your browser.
  • **.pdf** works in any PDF reader.
  • **.xlsx** open with Excel/Google Sheets; **.csv** importable anywhere.
