How do I access my orders and receipts?

Last updated: 2025-09-13

How do I access my orders and receipts?

  1. Sign in → **My Account → Orders**.
  2. Open an order to see items, download links, and the receipt.
  3. To re‑download files, click **Download** next to the item.

If you checked out as a guest, use the download links in your email receipt or contact lizukandu@gmail.com to add the order to your account.
